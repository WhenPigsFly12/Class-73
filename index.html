Campus Life

School Auditorium

Was established in 2009

The MPR(Multi-purpose room) or auditorium was built in 2009, the same year that Stonegate Elementary was established and it has space to fit 180 people!



School Library

Was built in 2010

The School Library was finished in 2010, the year right after the school was founded and our library has many books of all kinds!



School Grass Field

Was built in 2010

The School Grass Field was finished in 2010 around the time when the School Library was finsihed and it used to play many fun sports like soccer, football, or just running around and playing tag!
